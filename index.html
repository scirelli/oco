Provincial Instability Over Time
Data Visualization that depecits assesed instability of world provinces over time. Leidos has developed a methodology to transform stability metrics provided by the Global Database of Events Language and Tone (GDELT) to create a unique and valueable analytic that provides PXP users an ability to monitor global instability and maintain situational awareness at the sub-national level for every province in the world in near-real time.
Geographic Distribution of Refugee Flows
Data Visualization overlaying the number of news reports concentrated on the refugee crises in Syria,with the tone of those articles. Intersecting tone and news coverage provides PXP users rapid ability to infer how local populations perceive refugees and potentially the willingness of the local popualtion to accept the settlement of refugees.
Small Sat Imagery
Leidos is partnering with small satellite companies like Urthecast and Planet Labs to infuse small sateliite imagaery into the Advanced GEOINT Integrated Command Center (AGICC) solution to fuse PAI and open source analytics into fundamental GEOINT concepts, missions and capabilities.
ISIL Tweets Per Wilaya
Data Visualization depicting the number of tweets in Arabic and English per ISIL province (wilaya). PXP users can leverage the bar chart to rapid identify trending ISIL wilaya's and determine if an increase in tweets for a particular wilaya is due to a specific event, operation or announcement.
ISIL Blogosphere
Data Visualization depicting the most recent blog article captured by PXP using a real-time web crawler, captures ISIL blogs and webpages in real-time as they are disseminated. PXP captures all the text, metadata and images from a webpage or blog article and leverages the data for more advanced analytics, like image classification.
